What is Physical Intelligence?

Physical intelligence refers to the ability of robots to perceive, understand, and interact with their physical environment. This involves developing robots that can manipulate objects, navigate through spaces, and learn from their experiences. Physical intelligence is a crucial aspect of robotics, as it enables robots to perform tasks that require physical interaction, such as assembly, manufacturing, and healthcare.

Physical intelligence is built on the foundation of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and robotics. It requires the development of sophisticated algorithms and sensors that can process and interpret data from the physical environment. This data can come from various sources, including cameras, lidar, and tactile sensors.
