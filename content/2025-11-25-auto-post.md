The Indian AI ecosystem is characterized by:
* A large and growing talent pool of AI engineers and researchers
* A thriving startup culture, with many AI-focused startups emerging in recent years
* Government initiatives to promote AI adoption and innovation, such as the National AI Strategy
* Increasing investments in AI research and development from both domestic and international sources
