* **Rise of explainable AI**: As AI becomes more pervasive, there will be a growing need for explainable AI, which provides transparency and accountability in AI decision-making.
* **Growing importance of AI ethics**: The development and deployment of AI solutions will require a greater emphasis on ethics, with companies and organizations needing to ensure that their AI systems are fair, transparent, and unbiased.
